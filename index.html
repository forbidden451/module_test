About Us

Cafe Middle East is a breakfast restaurant located in the heart of the city, offering a warm and inviting atmosphere for a delightful breakfast experience. They are passionate about crafting extraordinary breakfast dishes that are visually appealing and bursting with flavors. Their menu caters to all taste preferences and dietary requirements, featuring classic Indian breakfast staples and international flavors sourced from local farmers and suppliers. They prioritize the use of wholesome ingredients to ensure high quality. The restaurant also offers an extensive selection of beverages, including specialty coffees and energizing smoothies. The staff provides friendly and attentive service to ensure a memorable dining experience. Cafe Middle East believes that breakfast should be a celebration of flavors, a moment of relaxation, and a time to connect with loved ones.
